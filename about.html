. ABOUT EIDO .

eido is a music producer also known as JFUL who has been producing since 2016. JFUL is more focused on lyricless instrumentals and beats whereas eido is more dedicated towards complete songs. Using vocal layers to create a certain melody and vibe with hard hitting sub-bass and filtered kicks in the low range are all components of the tracks that eido creates as an artist and the taste of music that he has.
