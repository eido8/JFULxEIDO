. IMPORTANT PRICING INFORMATION .

*NOTE: The Premium purchase ONLY applies to purchases on beats by JFUL (not eido) to be claimed ownership. Songs by eido cannot be claimed ownership and are limited to the Basic and Standard purchase. If you have accidentally made a Premium purchase for a eido track, contact eido & JFUL via the contact information at the bottom of the page and the money will be refunded to you. Tracks with watermarks will have them removed in your download file after purchase.

PURCHASE INSTRUCTIONS

Read the pricing information below and make your purchase using the paypal button below. After your purchase contact JFUL / eido via email using the email icon at the bottom of the page with your name, email, and purchase information (choice of plan, whether it is Basic, Standard, or Premium) and specify the song(s) that you would like to receive based on your purchase. Once your purchase information has been verified, JFUL / eido will get back to you within 48hours with the corresponding files via email you provided.
